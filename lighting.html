Recessed Lighting

Recessed lighting is highly favored in many homes, as it provides a streamlined, clean, modern look. They can be used in a targeted way, to highlight a work of art, or to illuminate an entire room.

Wishing for a gentle, room-filling glow in your washroom? Do you want to enhance the existing lighting in your living room? Recessed lights come in many different trims and types, allowing for all budgets and individual tastes.

Undercabinet Lighting

When you need direct, effective light for food preparation or cooking, undercabinet lighting is the solution of choice. It dramatically enhances the functionality and ambiance of your kitchen workspace.

Coming in many different types such as xenon, fluorescent, low voltage and highly-efficient LED, undercabinet lighting provides crisp illumination that a ceiling or pendant light fixture simply can’t match.

Security Lighting

Tips for Security Lighting

Let us help protect your property and your family with these best practices:

1

Placement matters: Place lights high on walls around the perimeter of your property. This will help stop bulbs from being tampered with, removed, or broken.

2

Focus on access points: Emphasize lighting at all entry and exit points from your home and property, eliminating hiding spots.

3

Use motion sensors: Motion sensor lights used in conjunction with permanent lighting provides an extra blanket of security on darker portions of your property, startling unwanted guests.

4

Consider off-grid options: Battery-powered lights, recharged via solar power, are an excellent available option for areas where wiring is difficult.
