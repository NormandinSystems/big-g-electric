Be the Envy of Your Neighbours!

With outdoor landscape lighting, at night your otherwise dark garden will come alive and what you couldn’t see before becomes the focus of your attention. Landscape lighting allows you to spend your after-work hours outdoors comfortably.

Specializing in Ambiance, Safety & Security

Your landscape lighting system will improve your safety and security while giving that much needed curb appeal. Our process begins by trying to get an idea of your primary reasons for deciding to light your landscape.

We ask important questions to help us design your space:

  • How often do you use the space?
  • What are your favourite features of your garden?
  • What mood would you like to achieve in the space?
  • What variety of lighting will best define your space?
  • What maintenance can be expected in the coming years?

Our main focus is to create two levels of light: one when inside and looking out, and the other when you are relaxing in the garden! Where possible, we believe that less is more, minimizing light pollution and glare. We then focus on the areas of your home that you are most proud of, decide what fixture is suitable while taking into consideration safety and security.

Next, we decide where our lights will be powered from and cable runs while trying to minimize damage to your garden. Lastly, once lights have been powered and adjusted, we schedule a night time visit to ensure that lights are pointed correctly and that everything is to your complete satisfaction!
