The Internet of Things

As technology and internet capabilities have improved, home automation has evolved rapidly. Now everyday items have an internet connection, creating the "Internet of Things" (IoT).

It is important to entrust the implementation of these automated residential systems to experts. At Big G Electric, we continually update our skills and certifications to ensure your home remains on the cutting edge of energy-efficient technologies.

Conveniently Control Your Home

Home automation means you’ll be able to remotely control several key parts of your home, such as heating and lighting, directly from an app.

This will save you significant money on electricity bills by ensuring you never accidentally leave devices on. These are subtle, but incredibly important ways to modernize your home. Plus, it’s an exceptionally easy way to keep an eye on your property.

Home Safety From Your Smartphone

Home automation is a major asset for your home security. From your smartphone, you can check that no door has been opened in your absence, turn on security lights as soon as it gets dark, and even watch the cameras installed on your property.

This level of control and awareness provides a sense of profound security for your home you may not have known you were missing.
