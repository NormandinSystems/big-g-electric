Benefits of a New Generac Standby Generator

By choosing a Generac standby generator from Big G Electric, you ensure reliable power backup, professional service, and long-term value for your home.

Reliability

Generac generators are known for their reliability and ability to provide continuous power during outages, ensuring your home remains functional.

Automatic Operation

These generators automatically detect power outages and start up within seconds, providing a seamless transition and minimizing disruption.

Flexible Fuel Options

Generac generators can run on natural gas or liquid propane, providing flexibility in fuel choice and ensuring a steady supply of power.

Protection for Appliances

By providing a stable power source, these generators protect your sensitive home appliances and electronics from damage caused by power surges.

Increased Home Value

Installing a standby generator can dramatically enhance your home’s value and appeal to future buyers, making it a valuable long-term investment.

Total Peace of Mind

Have peace of mind knowing your home will remain powered during extreme weather emergencies, protecting your family’s comfort and safety.
