Grants and Tax Credit Programs in Quebec

Quebec offers a variety of grants and tax incentives for homeowners and businesses looking to upgrade their electrical, ventilation, and heating systems.

Explore Your Options

Make Energy Efficiency Affordable

These provincial programs are designed to promote energy efficiency, reduce greenhouse gas emissions, and significantly improve your indoor air quality—all while putting money back in your pocket.

ÉcoPerformance Program

Provides financial assistance for energy-efficient upgrades in commercial, industrial, and residential sectors. Eligible projects include ventilation, heating system upgrades, and heat recovery installations that result in documented energy savings.

Rénoclimat

Offers subsidies for energy-efficient home renovations, including ventilation and heating system improvements. Homeowners can receive financial assistance after undergoing an energy audit that identifies eligible upgrades.

Chauffez vert

A financial assistance program that encourages the replacement of oil or gas heating systems with electric or other renewable energy options. This program aims to reduce fossil fuel consumption and improve energy efficiency.

Tax Credits for Home Renovation

Homeowners may be eligible for provincial tax credits when performing eco-friendly renovations. This includes installing energy-efficient ventilation systems or upgrading heating equipment to reduce energy consumption.
