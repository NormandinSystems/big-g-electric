Our Premier Partnerships

Collaborating with industry leaders to bring you the best materials, technology, and savings.

Hydro Quebec

As an official Hydro Quebec aggregator, we make saving money completely hassle-free. There's no need for you to fill out complicated forms or wait weeks for a rebate check. We apply the Hydro Quebec subsidies directly to your project so you only pay your out-of-pocket portion, and we handle getting reimbursed by Hydro. This partnership also allows us to source discounted materials, shipped right to our Pointe-Claire warehouse, ensuring your project is completed quickly and affordably.

Novair Heat Pumps and AC

We're proud to partner with Novair to offer high-efficiency heat pumps built for Quebec winters. Their reliable, energy-saving systems paired with our expert installation will deliver dependable comfort all season long.

Financeit

We understand that electrical upgrades are an investment. We've partnered with Financeit to offer flexible, affordable payment plans so you can get your project done today and pay over time.

5-Year Warranty on All Our Electrical Services

The Longest Warranty in the Industry!

Did you know that the minimum legal warranty offered by merchants in Quebec is only one year? At Big G Electric, we go far beyond this standard to provide you with unmatched peace of mind.

Why a 5-Year Warranty?

Quality is at the core of everything we do. That's why we guarantee our work and services for a full 5 years. This warranty reflects our commitment to durable and reliable installations.

Details of Our Warranty

1 Work Coverage
  • All our work (contract-based or hourly) comes with a 5-year non-transferable warranty.
  • This warranty covers only our workmanship and installations, not the materials.
Example: If an electrical connection comes loose after our work, we'll repair it free of charge under the warranty.
2 Material Warranty
  • Installed materials are covered by the manufacturer's warranty.
