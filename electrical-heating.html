Efficient, Easy-to-Maintain Home Comfort

At Big G Electric, our electricians take your specific living environment into consideration to ensure you get the best advice on electric heating. We carry a large range of modern appliances and accessories to suit your needs, lower your utility bills, and maximize your comfort.

Baseboard Heaters

The most popular form of electrical heating in Quebec. Placed near windows or cold outside walls, baseboard heaters quickly bring the room to a comfortable temperature using efficient air currents. We carry a large range of baseboard heaters, ranging from 500-2500 watts per device.

A significant advantage is that they allow for independently heated rooms. This directly translates to savings on your utilities, as rooms used less often can be kept cooler, unlike central heating. They are also mostly maintenance-free!

Electronic Thermostats

Do you still have your old bimetal thermostat? Like many Montrealers, you would be better off turning it in for a state-of-the-art electronic thermostat, which is much more precise. You will see your electricity bill get lighter immediately.

Hydro Quebec currently offers a rebate on replacing your conventional bi-metal thermostats to electronic models! Big G Electric offers several programmable models so you can maintain set temperatures for predetermined hours.

Convection Heaters

Electric convectors are an extremely efficient heating solution. According to the principle of convection, cold air is aspirated through the grid at the bottom, then released through the top after being heated. The air temperature in the room warms up almost immediately. These systems are quiet, sleek, and do not dry out the ambient air.

Heated Floors

Electric heated floors are the ultimate in home comfort. They release gentle, enveloping heat that surrounds you and the room in a blanket of perfect warmth.

Underfloor heating works with a variety of floor coverings, making each installation a unique, custom touch to your home. As well, underfloor heating is almost entirely maintenance-free and, like baseboard heaters, allows for precise per-room temperature control.
