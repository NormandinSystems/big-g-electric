Comprehensive Inspection Checklists

See exactly what our licensed technicians evaluate during your annual visit to guarantee your system's performance and safety.

Heating – Air Conditioning – Ventilation

Furnaces

  • Airflow: Vacuum to remove any dust or debris on the motor and fan blades.
  • Electronics: Test and inspect all electronic components including transformers, capacitors, limit switches and sequencers.
  • Filters: Wash reusable filters, or replace disposable filters.
  • Clean: Clean and vacuum cabinet to remove dust and debris.

AC / Heat Pump

  • Coils: Wash indoor and outdoor coils.
  • Drains: Clean and test all condensate drains, including pumps.
  • Electronics: Inspect and test components, contactors, capacitors & switches.
  • Compressor: Verify connections and performance.
  • Refrigerant: Verification of charge to optimize efficiency.
  • Disconnect: Verify connections and functionality of outdoor disconnect.
  • Test: Make sure the unit is working correctly in all modes.

Mini Split Units

  • Indoor Unit: Remove/wash cover and filters. Install collection bag, fill with expanding cleaning foam to push out dirt from coil, fan, drain. Rinse, ensure drainage, dry and reassemble.
  • Outdoor Unit: Wash coil and casing. Inspect electronics and connections. Verify refrigerant charge. Verify outdoor disconnect. Test in all modes.

Air Exchangers

  • Wash the casing, drain pan and filters.
  • Wash the heat recovery core.
  • Vacuum to remove dust/debris in fan motors.
  • Clean the condensate drain.
  • Verify damper function, controller and fan operation.
  • Verify inlet/outlet are clear of debris.
  • Test – Verify all modes function correctly.

Humidifier Tune Up

  • Cleaning interior to remove scale, dust and debris.
  • Replace the filter.
  • Verify accuracy and functionality of the humidistat.
  • Verify functionality of the solenoid valve.
  • Clean the condensate drain to ensure proper drainage.

*Any replacement filters not provided by the client are charged in addition, subject to discount for plan members.

Electrical Inspection

Smoke / CO Detectors

  • Test Functionality
  • Check Expiry Date
  • Vacuum detectors to remove dust & debris
  • Replace batteries as required

Pool & Spa Equipment

  • Check sub-panel connections and breakers
  • Test any GFCI protection
  • Verify bonding system

Main Service Panel Inspection

  • Verify any surge protection devices attached.
  • Verify breakers for signs of overheating.
  • Verify functionality of all fault/ground fault breakers.
  • Install duct seal to prevent condensation from outside meter base.
  • Close openings to keep out fingers or "critters".
  • Verify main grounding system.
  • Tighten all breaker connections to manufacturer specs.

Heating, Lighting & Receptacles

  • Check heating, lighting and outlet functionality
  • Test functionality of GFCI & AFCI plugs
  • Check heaters and thermostats for normal functionality
  • Tension test performed on receptacles throughout the home
  • Check functionality of all lights/dimmers/switches

Once we have completed our maintenance, a detailed report will be provided listing any preventative recommendations, or suggestions to help you get the most out of your systems and prevent future issues.
