Did You Know?

As of January 1st 2024, it is illegal to install fossil fuel heating equipment (Oil) in your home in Quebec.

The new decree also bans the repair of heating systems running on fuel that are more than 20-years-old. Don't wait for an emergency breakdown in the middle of winter!
